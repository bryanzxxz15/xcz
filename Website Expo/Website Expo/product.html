Our Coffee Products

Black Coffee

Made from brewed coffee beans without any added milk or sugar. It’s simple and pure, with a strong, bold flavor.

Hot-Latte

Espresso with steamed milk and a light layer of foam on top. It has a creamy texture with a mild coffee taste, perfect for those who prefer a smooth, not-too-strong coffee.

Hot-Espresso

A concentrated form of coffee served in small amounts, typically just a shot or two. It’s very strong and full of flavor, with a rich, intense taste.

Flat White Coffee

Similar to a latte, but with a higher ratio of coffee to milk and a velvety texture from the microfoam.
It's smooth and strong with less froth than a latte.

Mocha

A mix of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream.
It's a sweet, rich coffee with chocolate undertones.

Hot-Matcha

A blend of steamed milk and matcha (green tea powder), sometimes with espresso added for extra caffeine.

Cold-Brew

Coffee brewed slowly in cold water for 12-24 hours.
It’s less acidic and smoother than regular iced coffee, with a naturally sweet flavor.

Iced-Coffee

Brewed coffee that’s chilled and served over ice.
It’s refreshing and can be served black or with milk, sugar, or flavored syrups.

Hazelnut

Coffee flavored with hazelnut syrup or hazelnut extract, giving it a nutty, sweet taste that complements the richness of coffee.

Cold-Matcha

Iced matcha tea, often combined with milk or sweeteners,
resulting in a chilled drink with a smooth, grassy flavor.

Cold-Macchiato

Espresso, chocolate syrup, and milk served cold over ice, often topped with whipped cream.
It’s a sweet and indulgent coffee-chocolate combination.

Hot-Chocolate

A comforting, sweet drink made with milk and melted chocolate, often topped with whipped cream or marshmallows.
It’s rich and creamy, a great non-coffee option.

Iced-Mocha

Espresso, chocolate syrup, and milk served cold over ice, often topped with whipped cream.
It’s a sweet and indulgent coffee-chocolate combination

Iced-White Chocolate

Similar to iced mocha, but with white chocolate syrup instead of regular chocolate.
It’s a sweeter, creamy coffee drink.
